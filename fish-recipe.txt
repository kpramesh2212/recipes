Ingredients:
 - 1 kg fish
 - 1 tbsp coriander
 - 1 tbsp chilli powder
 - 1 tsp turmeric
 - 1 tbsp ginger paste
 - 1 tbsp garlic paste
 - 1 tsp lemon juice
 - 1 tbsp curd or yoghurt
 - 1 tbsp pepper powder
 - salt (to taste)

 Description:

1. Mix all ingredient together and set aside for 2 hours
2. Fry the fish in pan
